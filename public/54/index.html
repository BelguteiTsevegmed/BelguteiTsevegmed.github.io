Muzio Gambit - Wikipedia In chess, the Muzio Gambit, sometimes called the Polerio Gambit, is an opening line in the King’s Gambit in which White sacrifices a knight for a large lead in development and attacking chances. It begins with the moves: 1. e4 e5 2. f4 exf4 3. Nf3 g5 4. Bc4 g4 5. 0-0. White offers a knight, aiming to exploit Black’s weakness on the f-file to attack the black king.) link: (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muzio_Gambit)
